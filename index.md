# Azurra – Account and Data Deletion

Azurra is a social media application that allows users to create accounts, share posts, and interact with other users.

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their **Azurra account** and how they can delete **specific data** without deleting their entire account.

---

## Permanent Account Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their Azurra account and all associated data.

### Steps to delete your account

1. Open the **Azurra** mobile app.
2. Navigate to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion request.

Once the request is confirmed, the user's account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

### Data that will be deleted

When an account is deleted, the following data will be removed:

* User profile information (username, email address, profile photo)
* User posts and uploaded content
* Comments created by the user
* Likes and interactions
* Followers and following relationships

### Data retention

Some limited information such as **security logs or system records** may be retained for up to **30 days** for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes. After this period, the data will be permanently deleted.

---

## Partial Data Deletion

Azurra also allows users to delete certain data without deleting their entire account.

### Data users can currently delete

Users can remove the following data directly within the Azurra app:

* Delete their own comments
* Remove likes from posts (unlike)
* Unfollow other users

### Steps to delete specific data

1. Open the **Azurra** mobile app.
2. Navigate to the content or interaction you want to remove.
3. Tap the **delete**, **unlike**, or **unfollow** option.
4. Confirm the action.

The selected data will be permanently removed from the platform.
